01
Concept & Mood

This is a Juneteenth block party, a big reunion. The energy is joy, community, and summer celebration with a heavy dance floor focus. Think cookout meets outdoor festival meets cultural celebration. Warm, alive, electric. People are dancing, laughing, linked up.

"The vibe should feel like the best day of the summer."
02
Venue Inspiration

Victoria Yards is a shipping container yard in Charlotte, similar to The Bullpen in DC. Use the industrial container architecture as a backdrop or framing device. Stacked containers, corrugated metal walls, string lights, open sky. This grounds the event in the actual space and makes the design feel specific, not generic.

03
Creative Direction

We're giving you full creative freedom on visual style and typography. Make it stand out, make it memorable, make it yours. The one ask: it should feel unique and intentional, not like a template. This event means something culturally, and the design should reflect that.
